About the Program

âœï¸

The School Readiness Project (SRP) provides services for families of preschool aged children throughout O'ahu, Moloka'i, and Hawai'i Island. We provide developmental, social-emotional, hearing, vision, and autism screenings. SRP works to strengthen families through case management and parent education.
